HOW TO PLAY

TO MOVE AROUND

1. Press left arrow key to move in left direction

2. Press right arrow key to move in left direction

TO JUMP UP AND MOVE DOWN

1. Press up arrow key to jump

PLATFORM

1. It is a safe surface to jump on to protect from toxic substance and enemy

GRASS

1. Walk past the grass

2. If you jump over it, it will poison you and will reduce health by 1

SPIKES

1. Do not jump or walk over spikes as it will reduce health by 1

2. Jump over platform to avoid deduction of health

BUG

1. It can toxicate you faster than spikes and grass, will reduce health by 1

2. To save the protagonist, jump over the bug to kill
